Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Gyanloop

How is Gyanloop different from a regular engineering college?+
Gyanloop offers an industry-integrated curriculum built by 100+ leaders from Google, Microsoft, OpenAI, etc. We focus on project-based learning, real-world applications, and early industry exposure.
What is the difference between CS & AI and AI & Business?+
CS & AI is deep technical — you'll build complex systems and master AI/ML. AI & Business combines technical skills with product thinking and business strategy, ideal for those who want to build AI products and lead.
Can I apply for both programmes simultaneously?+
Yes, you can rank your preferences during the application process. You'll be considered for your top choice based on your profile and interview performance.
Do I need coding experience before joining?+
No prior coding experience is required. Our curriculum starts from the fundamentals and progressively builds advanced skills. A strong aptitude for problem-solving and curiosity is key.
What is the NSET exam and how do I prepare for it?+
NSET is our entrance test designed to assess logical reasoning, mathematical ability, and analytical thinking. We provide free mock tests and preparation material after you apply.
